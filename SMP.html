Server Rules & Etiquette

1. Respect All Players

Be kind and courteous. No hate speech, harassment, or excessive toxicity. We're here to build, not to break spirits.

2. No Griefing or Stealing

Do not destroy, deface, or steal from other players' builds or chests. This is a collaborative environment.

3. No Cheating or Exploiting

The use of hacks, x-ray, or abusing game/mod bugs is strictly forbidden and will result in a permanent ban.

4. Build with Consideration

Be mindful of server performance. Avoid massive, unoptimized contraptions that cause lag. Clean up temporary structures.

5. PvP is Consensual

Do not engage in player-versus-player combat unless all parties agree to it. This is a primarily PvE server.

6. Use Land Claiming

Protect your property and respect the claims of others. Unclaimed areas are considered wild and may be subject to community projects.
