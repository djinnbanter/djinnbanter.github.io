ABOUT
DJINN

Hey there! I'm DJINN, and I'm all about creating those unforgettable nights on the dance floor, the ones filled with pure energy and good vibes. My journey started during lockdown, spinning tunes on Twitch and building an amazing community. Now, I'm bringing that same energy to live events, and I've been lucky enough to play at some incredible places like Furcation and Insomnia Gaming Festival. My sets vary through house music, with a healthy dose of 00s and 90s throwbacks that'll take you right back. Oh, and I can't resist throwing in some trance, DnB, and those classic "diva power" anthems that just make everyone lose it. As a proud member of the LGBTQ+ community, it's super important to me that my sets are a space where everyone feels safe, included, and totally free to be themselves.

ABOUT
ZIGGY

There are certain nights that call for a sound that's deeper, darker, and more intense. That's where you'll find ZIGGY. This is my personal project for the pup, furry, and kink communities I'm a part of. The music here is more underground—hard techno, hard dance, and DnB—and the sets are built for those specific, 'you had to be there' moments where the energy is just right.
